Slow Fashion

Start Watching

Fast fashion is the largest polluting industry in the world, after oil. How fast will we realise that what we really need to do is slow down? Supporting labels that advocate fair trade, ethical production, organic sourcing, and innovation in the fashion industry are choices you can make today that will change our world tomorrow. Let’s mend before we throw, let’s swap before we’re bored, let’s change what we can while we’re still here.
